Shakshuka

Home

Description

This hearty shakshuka recipe brings the rich, comforting flavours of North Africa and the Middle East straight to your kitchen. Poached eggs simmer gently in a spiced tomato and capsicum sauce, seasoned with garlic, cumin and paprika for a bold, warming depth. Perfect for breakfast, brunch or even a light dinner, it’s best served with crusty bread to scoop up every last bit of the sauce. Simple, satisfying and packed with flavour, this one-pan dish is as nourishing as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 (28-ounce can) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 6 large eggs
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 small bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 small bunch fresh parsley, chopped
